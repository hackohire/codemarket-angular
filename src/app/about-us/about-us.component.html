My Career & Business Goals

As a child growing up in rural India I was facinated by stories of mathameticians and scientists from far off lands that solved problems of humanities. That facination paved the path to do my engineering at one of the prestageous technical institute Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. Technology also became a way to help my mother and my siblings back in India. From there on I became curious about business world and went on to do my MBA at premier Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore. Since then my inetrest has always been applying my experince in technology and business to solve human problems. That interest brought me to USA to work at Amgen. In the past 20 years I honed my business skills working at both large corporations and also at tech startups. I found my purpose when I started a meal planning and delivery business the same year my father passed away from diabetes and Steven Jobs passed away from Cancer. Building my own small business gave me first hand experince of the struggles of local businesses. Small Business are passionate and work hard to solve problems in our local communities but they do not have the access to the technical solutions that can help scale their businesses. Equipped with that understanding I started codemarket to help local businesses scale their businesses using technical solutions that large corporations uses.
