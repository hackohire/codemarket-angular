My Career & Business Goals

As a child growing up in rural India I was fascinated by stories of mathematicians and scientists from far off lands that solved problems of humanities. That fascination paved the path to do my engineering at one of the prestigious technical institute Indian Institute of Technology, Madras. Technology also became a way to help my mother and my siblings back in India. From there on I became curious about the business world and went on to do my MBA at premier Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore. Since then my interest has always been applying my experience in technology and business to solve human problems. That interest brought me to the USA to work at Amgen. In the past 20 years, I honed my business skills working at both large corporations and also at tech startups. I found my purpose when I started a meal planning and delivery business the same year my father passed away from diabetes and Steve Jobs passed away from Cancer. Building my own small business gave me first-hand experience of the struggles of local businesses. Small businesses are passionate and work hard to solve problems in our local communities but they do not have access to the technical solutions that can help scale their businesses. Equipped with that understanding I started codemarket to help local businesses scale their businesses using technical solutions that large corporations use.
