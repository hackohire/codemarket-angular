Thursday Thoughts

While it’s true that, at times . . .

You may know sadness.

You may know frustration.

You may know restlessness.

You may know anxiety.

You may know stress.

You may know insecurity.

It is also true that the YOU WHO KNOWS sadness, frustration, restlessness, anxiety, stress, and insecurity is NEVER any of those things.

The YOU WHO KNOWS all of these, and every other possible feeling-state, is the infinite, open, empty, loving, unbreakably resilient, and permanently peaceful space in which all feelings arise and dissolve without a trace.

Thursday Thoughts

Here’s a cost-free, effort-free way to make a difference of epic proportions.

Turn your attention inward and recognize your true Self as:

  • Ever-present, always whole and complete, knowing AWARENESS;
  • The unlimited, imperturbable space in which all your experience appears and dissolves;
  • The boundless universe of infinite inner peace, unconditional love, and unbreakable resilience.

As you deepen in this understanding, you will relieve everyone you love, and the world at large, of the burden of the impossible task of making you happy.

April 30, 2020
Thursday Thoughts

Let’s begin a new self-conversation by reflecting on the following:

What is it that is ever-aware of all of your experience?

What is it that, while ever-aware of all your experience, is not itself an object to be found within your experience?

What is it that knows every quality of your experience — tense, restful, despairing, elated, and so on — yet is devoid of any qualities. (Otherwise, an even-greater Awareness would have to know the qualities of this one.)

Consider that this infinite and ever-aware presence is YOU, who you really are, and that this gentle reflection is merely the recognition of your own being.

And, as an absence of qualities is the same thing as an undisturbed neutrality, as true peace, consider this simple, gentle conversation an opportunity to recognize the limitless peace behind all experience.
